What is a Crypto Currency?

A Crypto Currency is a digital currency, which is an alternative form of payment created using enctryption algorithms

The use of Encryption technologies means that cryptocurrencies function both as a currency and as a virtal accounting system and to use cryptocurrencies you need a wallet.

What is DEFI?

Decentralized Finance (DEFI) dates back to the year 2018 when a group of software developers and Entrepeneurs were trying to decide what to call their movement of new breed financial services that would be automated, built on blockchain, and caable of stripping our traditional banks

Three years later and DEFI is a big business. A user with a crypto wallet can trade digital assets, get loans, or take out insurance, among many things. Some $90 billion of collateral is locked up in these services, and more than 10 million people have downloaded MetaMask, one of the most populr digital wallets used to open up access to these networks.

Why choose D&E?

The main aim or venturing into the world of Decentralized Finance is to understand and make money from the decentralized world, and that is what D&E stands for. D&E is a DEFI training community designed to teach and gets it's students financially free.

We at D&E would like to help in moving the world to a more Decentralized field and also help in preparing people to be mentally and financially capable to handle the movement.
